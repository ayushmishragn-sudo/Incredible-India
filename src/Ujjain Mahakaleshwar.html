About Ujjain

Ujjain, located in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh, is a city of immense religious and historical significance. It is one of the seven sacred cities (Sapta Puri) in Hinduism.


The Mahakaleshwar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas in India and a major pilgrimage site. The city is also famous for its Kumbh Mela, which attracts millions of devotees.

Famous Places

Mahakaleshwar Temple

A revered Jyotirlinga dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its Bhasma Aarti.

Kal Bhairav Temple

Dedicated to Kal Bhairav, an incarnation of Lord Shiva, known for its unique rituals.

Ram Ghat

One of the oldest ghats on the Shipra River, famous for evening aartis.
