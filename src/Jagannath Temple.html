About Puri

Puri, located in the state of Odisha, is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites in India. It is home to the famous Jagannath Temple, dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Lord Vishnu.


The city is known for its annual Rath Yatra (Chariot Festival), where millions gather to witness the divine procession. Puri's golden beaches and cultural heritage also attract tourists worldwide.

Famous Places

Jagannath Temple

A sacred temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath, part of the Char Dham pilgrimage.

Puri Beach

A tranquil beach offering golden sands and a spiritual ambiance.

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site located near Puri, renowned for its stunning architecture.
