About Tirupati

Tirupati, located in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations in India. The city is famous for the **Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple**, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.


The temple is situated on the Tirumala Hills and is known for its rich history, spiritual significance, and the massive number of devotees who visit each year. The city is also home to other temples, ancient traditions, and cultural festivals.

Famous Places

Venkateswara Temple

The world-renowned temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, one of the richest and most visited temples in India.

Sri Kaliamman Temple

A temple dedicated to the goddess Kali, located in the heart of Tirupati.

Srivari Mutt

An important religious center where devotees are provided with free meals and other services.
