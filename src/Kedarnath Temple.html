About Kedarnath

Kedarnath, located in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites for Hindus. The Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is part of the Char Dham Yatra and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.


Perched at an altitude of 3,580 meters, Kedarnath offers not only spiritual solace but also breathtaking views of the surrounding snow-capped mountains. The temple attracts millions of devotees each year, especially during the Char Dham pilgrimage season.

Famous Places

Kedarnath Temple

A historic temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its spiritual significance and stunning location.

Triund View

A scenic spot offering panoramic views of the Himalayan peaks, popular among trekkers.

Ganga Dass

A sacred site close to the temple, believed to be an important spot for spiritual practices.
